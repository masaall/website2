Overview of Nglambor Beach

Nglambor Beach is one of the beautiful beaches located in Yogyakarta, precisely in Gunungkidul Regency. As we all know, Gunungkidul has a myriad of beautiful beaches that are very interesting to visit. Despite the increasing number of visitors, the beauty of Nglambor Beach remains well-preserved. The journey to Nglambor Beach takes approximately 2 hours, covering a distance of 75 km from the City of Yogyakarta.

One of the popular activities at Nglambor Beach is snorkeling. Although the beach is located near the Indian Ocean, it is protected by two large rocks that break the waves, creating calmer, clearer, and safer waters for snorkeling. In addition to snorkeling, visitors can enjoy an exciting adventure by crossing a suspension bridge that spans the sea, leading to small hills perfect for trekking enthusiasts.

Furthermore, the beach is equipped with complete facilities, including local food stalls, toilets, and a prayer room (mushola), ensuring visitors have a comfortable and enjoyable experience.
