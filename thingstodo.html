Things To Do

Snorkeling

Dive into the crystal-clear waters and discover the vibrant marine life beneath the surface. Snorkeling offers an up-close look at colorful fish and stunning coral reefs.

Crossing The Suspension Bridge

Enjoy a thrilling adventure as you traverse the suspension bridge, taking in panoramic ocean views and feeling the refreshing sea breeze.

Picnicking

Relax on the sandy shores with a beach picnic. Enjoy a leisurely meal with the sound of the waves and the beautiful coastal landscape as your backdrop.
