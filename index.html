ABOUT NGLAMBOR BEACH

Nglambor Beach is a hidden gem located in Purwodadi Village, Tepus District, Gunung Kidul Regency, Special Region of Yogyakarta. Renowned for its crystal-clear waters and serene environment, this beach offers an idyllic escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Visitors can enjoy a range of activities, including snorkeling to explore vibrant marine life, relaxing on the soft sandy shores, and taking in breathtaking views of the surrounding natural beauty. With its peaceful ambiance and picturesque scenery, Nglambor Beach is the perfect destination for both relaxation and adventure.
